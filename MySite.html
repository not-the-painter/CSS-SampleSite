Hello.

My name is Bob. I play bass for the hard rock band Wyntr, based in Ottawa, Canada. We play all over eastern Ontario, from Ottawa to Kingston, Pembroke to Oshawa, and points in between. I also played bass and synths for the prog-metal studio project Ariel's Attic and have collaborated on many other projects including The Liquid Edge and Syborne. When I'm not playing music I'm usually messing around with computers.

My Skills.

"Works with computers"

I'm the Product Verification Team Lead for Graphite Switchers at Ross Video in Ottawa. I also design and maintain several websites including wyntrband.com and louisesenecal.com. I'm a math major from Carleton University and have a Python certification from Udemy.

Audio Engineering

I have been mixing audio for over 10 years. I mixed both Ariel's Attic albums ('Garden of the Gods' in 2015 and 'Tragic Tales' in 2018) and mastered the debut album by Wyntr ('Amps & Lamps' in 2021). I offer mixing and mastering services from my home studio running Logic Pro X (Sonic Scoop/Pro Audio Files certification).
